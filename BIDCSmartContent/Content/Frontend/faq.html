Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What isBIDC MOBILE BANKING CAMBODIA service?

The service BIDC MOBILE BANKING CAMBODIA isBank services on smart mobile phones by BIDC provides personal customer support to perform financial transactions, non-financial and utilities improve

2. What is the main features of the BIDC MOBILE BANKING CAMBODIA service?

BIDC MOBILE BANKING CAMBODIA service offers the following key features:

  1. Querying account informationtransfer, save, loan
  2. Querying history information transfer, save, loan
  3. Transfer: Own account fund transfer, Fund transfer to other account
  4. Branch/ATM/CN location
  5. Look up exchange rate/ interest rate
  6. Open a saving account
  7. Settlement saving account

3. What is customers using the BIDC MOBILE BANKING CAMBODIAservice?

BIDC MOBILE BANKING CAMBODIA service allow all individual customers, including residents and nonresidents, bank accounts or no account at BIDC payment, registered or unregistered MOBILE BANKING CAMBODIA BIDCservice can use services at different levels.

    + Application setting BIDC MOBILE BANKING CAMBODIA on the smartphone / tablet
